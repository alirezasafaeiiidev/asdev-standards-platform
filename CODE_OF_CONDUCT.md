# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes, not people.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated disruptive behavior in discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
